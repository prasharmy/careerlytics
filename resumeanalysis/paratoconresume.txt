Resume Content Validation Function (is_valid_resume):
Resume Indicators: Checks for resume-specific keywords like:
Education: education, university, degree, bachelor, master, phd
Experience: experience, work, employment, job, company, professional
Skills: skills, technical, programming, software, technologies
Structure: summary, objective, profile, achievements
Non-Resume Detection: Identifies and rejects documents containing:
Business documents: invoice, receipt, contract, agreement
Academic papers: research paper, thesis, abstract, methodology
Legal documents: privacy policy, terms of service, legal notice
Financial documents: balance sheet, tax return, invoice
Medical records: medical record, prescription, diagnosis
Strong Pattern Rejection: Immediately rejects documents with:
ISBN numbers (books)
DOI identifiers (academic papers)
Academic volume/issue numbers
Research methodology sections
Structural Validation:
Minimum Requirements: At least 100 characters and 3 resume indicators
Contact Information: Phone number or email address presence
Date References: Years or months indicating work/education timeline
Enhanced Scoring: More indicators needed if contact info/dates are missing
User-Friendly Error Messages:
Clear Error Display: Shows detailed error messages in the upload form
Helpful Guidance: Provides specific requirements for valid resumes
Visual Feedback: Uses color-coded alerts with appropriate icons
