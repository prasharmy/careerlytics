Communication & Expression (30%)

| Parameter    | What AI Measures                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| Clarity      | Are thoughts logically structured?         |
| Fluency      | Smooth speaking without long pauses        |
| Vocabulary   | Professional and role-appropriate language |
| Grammar      | Sentence correctness                       |
| Articulation | Easy to understand pronunciation           |
| Conciseness  | Not too long, not too short                |

Confidence & Presence (20%)

| Parameter              | What AI Measures                    |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Confidence             | Hesitation, filler words, certainty |
| Tone Stability         | Calm vs nervous                     |
| Eye-contact (if video) | Looking at camera                   |
| Answer Certainty       | No self-contradiction               |
| Assertiveness          | Not too passive                     |

Thinking & Problem Solving (25%)

| Parameter         | What AI Measures            |
| ----------------- | --------------------------- |
| Logical Flow      | Situation → Action → Result |
| Critical Thinking | Reasoned answers            |
| Decision Making   | Clear choices               |
| Creativity        | Original thinking           |
| Stress Handling   | Quality under time pressure |

Professional & Role Fit (25%)

| Parameter            | What AI Measures        |
| -------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Professionalism      | Formal tone, no slang   |
| Honesty              | Consistency with resume |
| Role Motivation      | Why this job            |
| Culture Fit          | Team, ethics, attitude  |
| Leadership Potential | Ownership & initiative  |

HR Total Score Formula

HR Score =
0.30 × Communication
0.20 × Confidence
0.25 × Thinking
0.25 × RoleFit
